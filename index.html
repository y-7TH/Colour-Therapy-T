What Is Color Therapy?

Colour therapy, also know as chromothreapy, is a from of therapy that uses color and light to treat certain metal and physical health conditions.We can trace this from of therapy back to the ancient Egyptians. They made use of sun-filled room with colored glasses for therapeutic purposes.

Type of Colour Therapy?

In color therapy, different color are thought to be able to treat various conditions. Color therapists believe that a lack of the particular color in your body might be responsible for your living condition. Here’s a list of the most common colors used during color therapy and how we can use them.

  • Red:Red is used to energize or invigorate a person who might be felling tired or down.However,red may also trigger people who might always be tense,which is why it's used carefully in color therapy

  • Blue Chromatherapists use blue to treat depression and pain. Duter shades of blue are also thought to have sedative properties and may be helpful for people who experience insomnis or other sleeping disorders.

  • Green: Green is the color of nature, and according to chromatherapists, it can help relieve stress and relax a person.

  • Yellow: Yellow can be use to improve your mood and make you happy optimistic.

  • Orange: Orange, much like yellow,can be used to elicit happy emotions from people. The bright warm color is also thought to be able to stimulate appetite and mental activity.
